Why evaluate the subway option?

Project Focus

We evaluate a proposed subway option along this corridor.

By 2040, the boulevard will be redesigned with one major transit mode— BRT, light rail, or subway—but the decision is still undetermined.

Our goal is to assess the overall impacts of the subway option and provide clear, objective evidence for decision-making.
