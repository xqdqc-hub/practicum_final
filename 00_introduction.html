Roosevelt Boulevard Subway Sustainability Viewer

A compact visual guide to evaluate how a proposed subway corridor may improve green-space accessibility and equity along Roosevelt Boulevard.

Two questions guide the website: who is served, and how much access improves?
Project Overview

Evaluating the subway proposal through sustainability, green access, and equity.

This website assesses the proposed Roosevelt Boulevard subway scenario in two connected dimensions: green space accessibility and equity. It compares the existing bus-only condition with the proposed subway condition, then identifies priority tracts for future investment.
